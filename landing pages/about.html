About The Liquor Brothers

The Liquor Brothers is a tech-enabled liquor distribution company committed to sourcing, importing, and delivering authentic, verifiable alcoholic drinks from reputable international brands to both B2B and B2C customers in Nigeria, starting with high-demand urban areas on Lagos Island, in Lagos State. We address the growing public health risk of adulterated and fake alcoholic drinks that has gradually infiltrated the Nigerian market by providing a reliable, tech-supported solution built on trust, traceability, and convenience. What sets TLB apart as a unique Liquor Brand? The Liquor Brothers is more than just a liquor business—it's a trust revolution in an industry that desperately needs one. In Nigeria, the market is flooded with adulterated drinks that are not just fake but potentially fatal. We're solving that by importing only verifiable, top-tier liquor brands and leveraging technology to guarantee authenticity. It's app-driven, logistics-enabled, and intentionally designed to serve both high-end individual buyers and businesses like lounges and event planners. We're starting with Lagos Island, but the goal is national reach. What sets us apart isn't just the product; it's the safety, the transparency, and the trust we're creating in every single bottle.

Our Mission

We aim to build trust in the Nigerian liquor market by providing a secure, technology-driven delivery model that ensures product authenticity and customer safety.

Why We Exist

  • ✔100% Verified & Imported Products
  • ✔Seamless, Safe Delivery to Any Location
  • ✔Digital Authentication for Every Bottle
  • ✔Trusted by Event Planners, Lounges, Hotels, and Individuals

Where We Operate

We're proudly headquartered in Victoria Island, Lagos, and actively expanding across other cities in Nigeria.
